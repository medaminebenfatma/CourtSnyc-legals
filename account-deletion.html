CourtSync Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-04-14

This page explains how CourtSync users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

App and developer

  • App name: CourtSync

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the CourtSync app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Open Settings / Help & Support.
  5. Tap the account deletion request option and submit the request.

If the in-app option is temporarily unavailable, users can request deletion by contacting support from the email address linked to the account.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile data (name, email, phone, location profile fields, demographics stored in profile).
  • Authentication/session associations linked to the account.
  • Device push-token registrations associated with the account.
  • Notification preference records tied to the account.
  • Other user-linked data that is not required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or accounting obligations.

What data may be retained

CourtSync may retain limited data where required for legal, security, anti-fraud, dispute resolution, or accounting obligations. In such cases, data is restricted and retained only as long as necessary for those purposes.

In match history/statistics contexts, references may be anonymized to preserve system integrity and historical consistency.

Processing timeline

  • Acknowledgement target: within 72 hours.
  • Standard completion target: within 30 days.

Contact

For account deletion requests or questions about personal data handling, contact CourtSync support through the app support channel.
